ABOUT THE COMPANY

Zoopla is a property marketplace focused on helping people make better property decisions. Data plays a central role in improving journeys for both consumers and estate agents, with measurement, experimentation, and insight seen as key drivers of product and commercial performance.


ROLE OVERVIEW

Zoopla is hiring a Data Analyst to partner closely with business stakeholders and support measurement, experimentation, reporting, and insight generation.

This role spans several analytics disciplines, including stakeholder partnership, experimentation analysis, data modelling, self-serve analytics, and collaboration with data science and product teams.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Own the data relationship with business stakeholders, with support from the wider Analytics team
- Bridge commercial and product discussions using data
- Report on baseline performance and trends across the business
- Support a self-serve analytics culture
- Analyse and report on complex experimentation
- Build strong analyst-stakeholder relationships so that data is embedded in decision-making
- Contribute across analytics disciplines, including:
  - data product design
  - data modelling
  - analytical deep-dives
  - support for data science, AI, and machine learning projects
- Contribute ideas and innovation across the team
