Senior Customer Marketing Data Analyst

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Utility Warehouse (UW) provides bundled utility services across energy, broadband, mobile, and insurance. The company’s aim is to make household services simpler and more cost-effective for customers.

UW is continuing to scale and is investing in data capabilities to support growth across marketing, product, and customer strategy.


ROLE OVERVIEW

UW is hiring a Senior Customer Marketing Data Analyst to work directly with the Customer Marketing team in a cross-functional environment.

This role sits between analytics engineering and business strategy. You will own the customer marketing data domain, develop robust data models, improve reporting and self-serve analytics, and translate data into strategic recommendations that influence campaign design and long-term marketing effectiveness.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Analytics Engineering:
- Build end-to-end data models using Dataform
- Handle complex transformations and maintain scalable, reusable datasets

Insight & Strategy:
- Move beyond reporting what happened to explaining why it happened
- Provide actionable recommendations that influence marketing decisions

Performance & Reliability:
- Optimise SQL for cost and runtime
- Investigate data quality issues and reduce technical debt

Dashboards & Reporting:
- Build self-serve dashboards and tools for KPI exploration
- Support users with access to trusted marketing datasets

Domain Ownership:
- Develop strong understanding of customer marketing data and objectives
- Help shape goals and priorities for the domain

Process & Collaboration:
- Work closely with stakeholders to refine requirements and manage delivery
- Mentor other analysts through code reviews and best practices


CANDIDATE PROFILE

- Strong hands-on experience as a senior data analyst or analytics engineer
- Excellent SQL skills, including debugging and performance optimisation
- Strong experience with data modelling and transformation tools such as Dataform or dbt
- Good knowledge of attribution modelling and cohort analysis
- Experience influencing technical guidelines and standards
- Familiarity with marketing platforms such as Braze, Optimizely, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or GA4
- Experience with Git / GitHub and modern analytics workflows
- Strong ability to communicate insight clearly and influence business outcomes
