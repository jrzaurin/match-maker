ABOUT THE COMPANY

Utility Warehouse (UW) provides bundled utility services including energy, broadband, mobile, and insurance, aiming to simplify everyday services for customers.


ROLE OVERVIEW

We are hiring a Data Engineering Manager to lead the data engineering team and define the technical vision for UW’s data platform.

You will own the full data lifecycle, from ingestion to analytics, and lead a major initiative to build a scalable enterprise data warehouse.


KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Leadership:
- Manage and mentor a team of data engineers
- Foster a culture of technical excellence and continuous delivery

Platform & Architecture:
- Define data platform strategy
- Lead transition toward a data mesh architecture
- Oversee ingestion, transformation, and analytics layers

Pipelines & Warehousing:
- Build and maintain ETL/ELT pipelines
- Implement scalable data models (Kimball, Data Vault)

Infrastructure & DevOps:
- Ensure platform reliability using Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD
- Work with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform)

Collaboration:
- Partner with data architects and governance teams
- Support security, compliance, and data quality initiatives


REQUIREMENTS

- Proven experience managing data engineering teams
- Strong background in data engineering and SQL-heavy workflows
- Experience with cloud data platforms (BigQuery, Snowflake)
- Experience with dbt or similar tools
- Strong system design and distributed systems knowledge
- Experience with containerisation (Docker, Kubernetes)
