ABOUT THE COMPANY

PubX builds next-generation, publisher-first agentic advertising infrastructure.

Our platform uses machine learning to make real-time, revenue-critical pricing decisions across programmatic advertising auctions. By analysing bid-stream data and historical patterns, we optimise pricing at the level of individual ad impressions, delivering measurable revenue uplift for publishers.

We are currently ranked #5 globally in Prebid Analytics Adapter Rankings and are a founding member of AgenticAdvertising.org, helping define the next generation of autonomous advertising systems.


THE PROBLEM

Digital publishers leave significant revenue on the table due to static or rule-based pricing systems. While every ad impression is unique, most platforms treat them identically.

PubX addresses this by applying machine learning to optimise pricing decisions in near real-time across billions of auction events.
